About Astrologer Love Guru

Astrologer Love Guru Pvt Ltd was founded in 1985 by Mr. Arun Bansal. He holds an M.Sc. in Physics from Delhi University and has also completed M.Phil & Research in Computer Science from Jawaharlal Nehru University, Delhi. Mr. Arun Bansal developed the first comprehensive astrology software in India in 1978, introducing modern techniques of mathematics and computers to astrology.

The basic aim of the company

  • To promote and standardize astrology and other allied sciences internationally.
  • To serve mankind by utilizing science and raising awareness of developments in the field.
  • To promote research in astrology and other predictive sciences.

To achieve its aim, Astrologer Love Guru has released a series of astrological software starting from DOS technology to Windows, XP, and Vista. It has also developed complete astrology software for Palm organizers based on Palm OS and Microsoft Mobile Windows, providing astrologers with a complete astrological tool in their pocket, useful for casting horoscopes anywhere, anytime.

Astrologer Love Guru publishes Future Samachar, a monthly astrological magazine in Hindi since 1995, containing articles on astrology popular all over India and abroad. It also invited societies from all over India to form the All India Federation of Astrologers Societies in 2001, which has established more than 100 chapters in India and abroad.

Astrologer Love Guru offers courses on astrology, Vastu, palmistry, numerology, and Tarot, with over fifteen thousand students having learned these subjects. It publishes the bilingual quarterly magazine Research Journal of Astrology to promote research in the field. Astrologer Love Guru also provides genuine remedial and spiritual products under one roof, and has published over 30 titles on astrology, Vastu, palmistry, Lal Kitab, remedies, and numerology.

Astrologer Love Guru provides personal consultancy on astrology, palmistry, and Vastu in person and online. To promote astrology, Astrologer Love Guru has successfully organized International Astrological conferences in Mauritius, Netherlands, Bangkok, and Singapore. At the national level, it organizes many conferences all over India. Nakshatra exhibition is also organized by Astrologer Love Guru with India Trade Promotion Organisation (ITPO) every year in Pragati Maidan from the last five years.
